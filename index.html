The Cow

An Eassy About Cow

A cow is cattle that eat grass and husk.Cow gives us Milk. They are generally white, black or brown-skinned. In India Jersey Breed of a cow is considered as the finest breed. Across the world, there is a total of 920 breeds of cows are yet recognized. Young Cows are called as ‘Heifer’. A cow’s sound is called ‘Moo’. Sadly, due to lack of space cows are often seen roaming around on streets. They harm themselves by consuming plastics and other harmful things from trash. Cow skin is used for leather belts, purse, etc. Cow meat is also one of the biggest contributors to the meat industry around the world. In India, the killing of a cow is a very sensitive issue. Apart from the religious point of view, killing a cow is an incessant act. An innocent animal with such benefits is not meant to kill for edible or commercial purposes. The killing of cows is always a debatable concept in India. Through some events, it is found that some innocent lives are taken by the accusation of killing cows.
